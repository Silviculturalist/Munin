static below_ground_biomass(species, root_detail, **kwargs)[source]¶

Dynamically selects and evaluates the appropriate below-ground biomass function.

Parameters:

  • species – ‘birch’, ‘pine’, or ‘spruce’.

  • root_detail – Resolution of smallest included roots (2 or 5 mm).

  • kwargs – Additional arguments required by specific functions.

Returns:

Below-ground biomass (g).
